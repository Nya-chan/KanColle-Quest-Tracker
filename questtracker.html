KanColle Quest Tracker

FAQ

Q: How to work with the tool?

A: Click on the quest and it will show you the quest chain up to it. Click it again to clear selection, or use the tab in the corner.

Q: How to mark completed quests?

A: Click the red cross in the corner to hide it as completed. Click the "Show deleted" tab in the corner to show these hidden quests to re-add them and click it again to hide them anew. Daily, weekly, monthly and quarterly quests get re-added automatically at 5AM JST.

Q: How does ToDo mode work?

A: If you have ToDo mode enabled - shows "ToDo ON" - then it won't remove clicked quest, just its prerequisities. Adding quests back still works the same.

Q: How about saving the data?

A: The data get saved into your browser when you click save or automatically when you leave the tab. You can also use the box below to save or load the quests manually.
